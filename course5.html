Diploma in Graphic Design

This diploma program equips you with essential graphic design skills, focusing on both theory and practical applications. You will learn to create visual content using industry-standard tools.

Course Objectives:

  • Understand design principles and color theory
  • Learn to use Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
  • Create logos, banners, and marketing materials
  • Explore typography and layout design
  • Build a professional portfolio

Duration:

6 months

Prerequisites:

No prior design experience is required.
